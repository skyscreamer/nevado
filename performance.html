Nevado+SQS is comparable speed-wise to ActiveMQ in persistent mode, which is to say it's not breath-taking. That said, the big advantage with SQS is this solution scales linearly. Add threads and boxes and you can scale your messaging horizontally and indefinitely.

Message Size

SQS imposes a message body maximum of 64k. Due to message serialization, this means an object upper-limit of 48k in Nevado. If you need larger payloads than this, we recommend that you store the payload in an external store like DynamoDB and use Nevado JMS to pass identifiers to the data. This is referred to as the Claim Check Pattern. Smaller messages improve performance.
